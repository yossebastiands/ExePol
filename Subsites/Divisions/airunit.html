Welcome to Air Unit

The Air Unitis the aerial backbone of law enforcement operations, providing critical overwatch, real-time intelligence, and rapid deployment capabilities. Operating above cityscapes and across remote terrain, Air Unit pilots are the eyes in the sky—coordinating with ground units to maintain control, pursue suspects, and keep our skies secure.

Mission

The Air Support Unit exists to elevate tactical efficiency by delivering high-altitude support for ground teams. From high-speed pursuits to search-and-rescue operations, air unit plays a crucial role in:

Core Responsibilities

  • Pursuit Overwatch– Maintaining visual on fleeing suspects and relaying route data.
  • Aerial Reconnaissance– Scanning areas for threats, missing persons, or suspect hideouts.
  • Scene Management– Monitoring large-scale incidents like protests, accidents, or roadblocks.
  • Search & Rescue– Assisting in locating missing civilians or officers in hard-to-reach areas.
  • Tactical Deployment– Inserting SWAT or specialized units via aerial transport.

Officer Requirements

Minimum Requirements:

  • Officer Rank
  • Clean internal record
  • Currently clear from any disciplinary or punishment
  • Familiarity with pursuit tactics and advanced driving
