Law Enforcement & Forum Guide

Law Enforcement Basics

As a Law Enforcement Officer in Executive Roleplay, you are expected to maintain order, enforce laws, and act professionally during every situation. Below are the basic responsibilities:

  • Understand and follow the chain of command.
  • Always carry your badge and identification.
  • Maintain radio discipline; keep communications clear.
  • Follow proper arrest and Miranda procedures.
  • Ensure the safety of civilians during pursuits and operations.

Failure to follow these rules may result in internal investigations or disciplinary action.

How to Use the Forum

The LEO Hub provides a centralized platform for information and updates. Here’s how to navigate:

  • Announcements: Read the latest updates about LEO operations.
  • Guides: Learn LEO basics and procedural workflows.
  • Departments: Access department-specific resources like LSPD, LSCS, SASPR.
  • Services: Submit requests like reimbursements, transfers, or reporting absence.
  • Forums: Public boards and update trackers for community discussions.
