Los Santos Police Department

The Los Santos Police Department (LSPD) is the primary law enforcement agency within the city of Los Santos. We are committed to protecting the lives and property of citizens through professionalism, fairness, and discipline. Our officers uphold justice, enforce the laws of San Andreas, and strive to maintain peace and order throughout the city.

Whether patrolling city streets, responding to 911 calls, engaging in high-stakes pursuits, or participating in community events, our duty is to serve with integrity, courage, and accountability.

What We Offer

  • Structured rank system with promotions and divisions
  • Realistic police procedures (traffic stops, investigations, arrests)
  • Specialized units: SWAT, GIU, HEAT, HSIU, Mary Unit, Air Unit
  • Ongoing training programs and situational awareness
  • Opportunities for leadership, undercover work, and more

Requirements

  • You are already an LSPD officer
  • Must understand and respect the chain of command
  • Ability to stay calm and professional in tense situations
  • Willingness to learn protocols and handle situations maturely
