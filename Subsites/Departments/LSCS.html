Los Santos County Sheriff

The Los Santos County Sheriff (LSCS) serves as the primary law enforcement agency for the greater Los Santos County region, including rural areas, small towns, and unincorporated zones beyond city limits. We are dedicated to upholding the law with honor, grit, and rural community presence, ensuring safety across all corners of the county.

Our deputies face unique challenges—from remote patrols and backroad traffic enforcement to gang interdictions and high-risk warrant executions. Every action reflects our values of discipline, respect, and professionalism.

Whether you’re assisting stranded civilians, engaging in tactical operations, or managing county jails, you are the backbone of law enforcement across Los Santos County.

What We Offer

  • Distinct rank structure and advancement path for deputies
  • Rural and tactical policing with realistic procedures
  • Specialized units: SEB, FAST, HEAT, HSIU, Mary Unit, Air Unit
  • Hands-on field training and situational drills
  • Opportunities in prison security and fugitive tracking

Requirements

  • Must be an active member of the LSCS or approved transfer
  • Strong understanding of chain of command and jurisdiction
  • Willingness to operate in county-specific scenarios
  • Maturity and professionalism in rural law enforcement situations
